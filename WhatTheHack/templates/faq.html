Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join this hackathon

This competition is open to anybody 16 years and above. Anyone below 18 years old will require their parents to sign an indemnity form in order to use the equipment.

What does it mean by "Ideas are open to interpretation"

It means that you can decide on how you want to argue your stance as. An example would be interpreting space as Outer Space (rockets, moon, planets) or Space in an architectural sense (A space we live in)

What is the maximum team size

4 members maximum.
