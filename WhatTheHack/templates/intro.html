What is What The Hack?
What The Hack @ SUTD is a 24 Hour hackathon that aims to bring together people of multiple disciplines and provides them with the resources to jumpstart their ideas.

What you get out of What The Hack:
  • Bring crazy ideas to life
  • Further develop on your ideas after the Hackathon with The MIT+SUTD Design Odyssey Programme
  • Learn to use advanced prototyping tools such as CAD modelling software, laser cutters and 3D Printers
  • Network with our sponsors!

Come solo or come as a team! Gather your fellow hackers into a team with a maximum team number of 4 or come down and form teams on the day itself! Your team can have a maximum of 4 members. Alternatively, Yyou can go at the theme's solo too! Who knows?! You might even be able to win a prize all by yourself!
