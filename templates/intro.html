What is What The Hack?
Awaiting Vector Image
What The Hack @ SUTD is a 24 Hour hackathon that aims to bring together people of multiple disciplines and provides them with the resources to jumpstart their ideas.

What you get out of What The Hack:
  • Bring crazy ideas to life
  • Further develop on your ideas after the Hackathon with The MIT+SUTD Design Odyssey Programme
  • learn to use advanced prototyping tools such as 3D modelling tools,laser cutters and 3D Printers
  • Network with our sponsors!
